Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the occupancy information is accurate? +

Occupancy is updated in real-time by verified students who are physically present at each location. Each update includes a timestamp so you can see how recent the information is. The more students who participate, the more reliable the data becomes.

What if my university isn't supported yet? +

We're continuously adding new universities. You can help by contacting your student affairs or IT department and asking them to partner with Is It Full. We'll work with them to get your campus set up quickly.

How often should I update occupancy levels? +

Update whenever you notice the occupancy has changed significantly. If you arrive at a location and the status seems outdated, take a moment to update it. Even occasional updates from various students throughout the day keep the information useful.

Do I need to create an account? +

Yes, you'll need to sign up with your university email address. This ensures that only verified students from your university can view and update occupancy information, keeping the data trustworthy and relevant.

What information do you collect? +

We only collect your university email for verification and basic account information. We don't track your location or which spaces you visit. Occupancy updates are anonymous‚Äîwe only show that a verified student updated the status.

Can I suggest new locations to add? +

Absolutely! We work with each university to determine which study spaces to include. If there's a popular study spot missing, let us know and we'll coordinate with your university to add it.

About Is It Full

The Problem

Every student has asked themselves: "Is the library full right now?" Walking to university only to find no available study space wastes time and disrupts your study plans.

The Solution

Is It Full puts real-time occupancy information in your hands. Check before you go, find alternative spaces, and contribute updates to help fellow students.

Community-Powered

This system only works because of students like you. By updating occupancy when you're at a location, you help everyone make better decisions about where to study.
